2. Add ginger, garlic, and red onions. Saute for 5 minutes until slightly

3.SCRAMBLED EGGS

6:STEPS TO MAKE SCRAMBLED EGGS

1.Beat the Eggs. Crack the eggs into the mixing bowl and Whisk in 3/4 teaspoon salt and a few grinds of pepper.
2.Prep the pan. Heat the nonstick pan over medium-low heat. Add butter to the Pan.
3.Add the eggs. Gently pour in the eggs. Pause to let them heat slightly .
4.Scramble the eggs constantly. Use the spatula to move the eggs acros light.
5.Add mix-ins. As the eggs start to set, add chopped fresh herbs,now s the time to cook it.
6.Serve the eggs. Gently spoon the eggs onto the warmed plates.
